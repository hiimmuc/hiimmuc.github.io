**Duration:** Nov 2021 – Sep 2023  
**Organization:** Viettel Big Data Analytics Center, Viettel Telecom · Hanoi, Vietnam

## Overview

Led the end-to-end development and production deployment of a large-scale speaker verification system for Viettel's call center — Vietnam's largest telecom operator. The system replaced traditional OTP and security-question authentication with voice biometrics, serving **millions of customers daily**.

## Key Contributions

- Designed and trained speaker embedding models on large-scale Vietnamese call-center audio data
- Built the full inference pipeline and deployed with **Triton Inference Server** for high-throughput, low-latency serving
- Reduced manual verification workload by **>50%** across Viettel's call center operations
- Achieved **≈99% accuracy** on internal Vietnamese speaker datasets, outperforming existing baselines
- Ranked **Top 20** in the VoxSRC 2022 international competition (beginning phase)
- Integrated data pipeline using **Kafka** for real-time audio streaming from call-center infrastructure

## Technologies

`Python` `PyTorch` `Triton Inference Server` `Docker` `Kafka` `Speaker Verification` `ASR` `VAD` `NLP`
