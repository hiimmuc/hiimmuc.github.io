## Overview

A three-stage research pipeline for personalizing the eHMI of autonomous personal mobility vehicles (APMVs) based on passenger personality — developed as part of the NAIST MEXT-funded robotics program.

## Three-Stage Pipeline

1. **Empirical Study** — Survey-based study to understand how personality traits influence passenger preferences for robot communication style (tone, speed, verbosity)
2. **Causal Discovery & Recommendation** — Causal graph modeling between personality factors and eHMI preferences; recommendation system to predict preferred voice profile per user
3. **Voice Cloning & Personalized eHMI** — Neural voice cloning to synthesize personalized robot voices; integration into the APMV eHMI interface

## Technologies
