**Duration:** Nov 2021 – Nov 2022  
**Organization:** Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), Hanoi, Vietnam  
**Supervisor:** Prof. Nguyen Pham Thuc Anh, HUST

## Overview

Developed a deep learning–based fire detection system to guide the navigation of an autonomous firefighting robot. The system processes real-time camera feeds, identifies fire and smoke regions, and outputs directional guidance to the robot's navigation stack.

## Key Contributions

- Implemented and benchmarked YOLO and SSD object detection models for fire and smoke detection
- Deployed the inference pipeline on NVIDIA Jetson Nano for edge inference with real-time constraints
- Collaborated with the Robotics and AI Lab at HUST

## Technologies

`Deep Learning` `YOLO` `SSD` `NVIDIA Jetson Nano` `Python` `Computer Vision`
